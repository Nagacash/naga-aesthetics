What should I do when my scalp micropigmentation fades?


For an optimal high-contrast appearance, scalp micropigmentation usually needs refreshing every two to five years. For most of our clients, this can be done in just one sitting – only in very rare cases is a second session needed.

Our pricing is designed in such a way that you’ll get an estimate beforehand of roughly how long your session will take. You’ll then make payment at the end of the session. The length of the Naga Aesthetics session will be roughly the same as one session of your original treatment.

Our experience enables us to make very realistic estimates of how long a session will take, so that there are no unpleasant surprises when you come to pay. The first refresher session will be needed after three to five years at the earliest.
